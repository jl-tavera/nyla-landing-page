Nyla is here to help you find your beloved four-legged friend. Just send a message via WhatsApp with some photos and a brief description of your dog. It's that easy! Thousands of people can join forces to search for your best friend. Together, we can make it happen

HELP OTHERS

Here are the steps to assist others with Nyla

  1. Reach out to Nyla via WhatsApp
  2. Capture a photo of any stray dog you come across on the street
  3. Cross-reference the photo with active reports
  4. Share the location where you found the dog and your contact information if necessary

By following these steps, you can help reunite lost pets with their owners through Nyla's platform. Let's make a difference together!
